# Dievas Family — Baltic Roster

> See also: [dievas_family_baltic.md](dievas_family_baltic.md)

## Command hierarchy

Dievas/Dievs holds supreme authority as sky-father and ruler of cosmic order. His sons — the Dievo sūneliai / Dieva dēli, also called Ašvieniai in their horse form — serve as celestial laborers and protectors, pulling Saulė's chariot and maintaining the heavenly farmstead. Saulė governs her own parallel household but cooperates closely with the Dievas family; her daughters (Saulės dukros / Saules meitas) interact with the sons as companions. Aušrinė, daughter of Saulė, serves as dawn herald and fire-kindler. Mėnulis/Mēness governs the night sky independently but is bound to the family through marriage to Saulė.

Perkūnas acts as enforcer of divine law with delegated authority from Dievas — not a subordinate, but an ally who punishes divine misconduct including within the Dievas family itself.

No formal rank structure among the sons is attested in primary sources; they function as an inseparable pair or collective.

## Individual distinctions — Primary sources

> **Named Collective note:** For Named Collective profiles, this section is mandatory — do not omit it even when individuals closely resemble the collective profile. Every named member should appear here with any trait that differs from (or adds to) the collective baseline.

**Dievas (Lith.) / Dievs (Latv.)**
- `[Specialization-signature]` Supreme cosmic authority — sole arbiter of celestial order; all other Baltic deities operate within the order he embodies; no equivalent authority is attributed to any other family member
- `[Characteristic]` Non-interventionist patriarch — holds supreme power but governs through established order rather than direct action; delegates enforcement to Perkūnas even when family members are the offenders
- `[Modifier]` Non-intervention principle — the sky-father's authority is upheld through established cosmic order rather than personal action; a recurring dispositional pattern attested across dainas and Prätorius, not a single event — *attested across dainas and Prätorius*

**Dievo sūneliai (Lith.) / Dieva dēli (Latv.) — the Heavenly Twins**
- `[Specialization-major]` Full animal transformation — manifest as white horses with golden manes (Ašvieniai); no other member of the family is attested with complete cross-species physical transformation
- `[Specialization-minor]` Maritime rescue — sail a golden boat to recover Saulė from the sea; the only members described navigating the sea realm as a primary feat
- `[Characteristic]` Anonymity — individual names are not attested in any primary source; they exist and act exclusively as an inseparable pair or collective unit throughout all surviving texts
- `[Strength]` Inexhaustible endurance — pull the celestial chariot across the sky daily without tiring; no fatigue is ever attributed to them

**Saulė (Lith.) / Saule (Latv.)**
- `[Specialization-signature]` Living embodiment of the sun — not a deity who commands the sun but a being whose existence is coextensive with the sun itself; her daily journey constitutes the day
- `[Strength]` Agricultural and seasonal sovereignty — her warmth and light govern the growth of crops and the turning of the seasons; human prosperity depends on her continued circuit
- `[Characteristic]` Parallel household — maintains her own celestial farmstead with her daughters (Saulės dukros / Saules meitas) adjacent to Dievas's; her independence as household-keeper makes her membership in the Dievas family ambiguous across sources
- `[Modifier]` Marital estrangement — her fractured marriage with Mėnulis (who committed infidelity with Aušrinė) is ongoing and unresolved; the permanent separation of sun and moon in the sky reflects their lasting conflict — *attested in Lithuanian dainos and Latvju dainas*

**Aušrinė**
- `[Specialization-minor]` Tripartite liminal form — alone among family members she is consistently attested in three distinct manifestations: a star in the sky realm, a maiden on land, and a mare in the sea realm; her triple form enables her unique cross-realm function as herald
- `[Characteristic]` Herald and fire-kindler — kindles the dawn fire and opens the sky gates for Saulė each morning; structurally the most junior member as daughter of Saulė rather than daughter of Dievas
- `[Modifier]` Entanglement with Mėnulis's punishment — named as the catalyst for the cosmic marital scandal; her infidelity with Mėnulis is the proximate cause of his disfigurement by Perkūnas — *attested in Lithuanian dainos and Latvju dainas*

**Mėnulis (Lith.) / Mēness (Latv.)**
- `[Characteristic]` Independent night domain — governs the night sky as a distinct cosmic realm; alone among family members his primary domain (night) is structurally opposed to the rest of the family's domain (day and dawn)
- `[Fault]` Punished status — the only family member explicitly depicted as having been physically disfigured by Perkūnas; his damaged face remains permanently marked as both moral reminder and cosmic explanation for lunar phases and eclipses
- `[Modifier]` Lunar disfigurement — face split or marked by Perkūnas as punishment for infidelity with Aušrinė; the visible phases and markings of the moon are attributed to this punishment — *attested in Lithuanian dainos and Latvju dainas*
