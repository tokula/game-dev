# Ragana — Baltic Roster

> See also: [ragana_baltic.md](ragana_baltic.md)

## Command hierarchy

Ragana is the apex authority in the witch-deity hierarchy. She commands lesser raganos ([raganos_baltic.md](raganos_baltic.md)) who gather under her at Winter Solstice. No being of equivalent or superior rank is attested over her in the witch-domain specifically; the structural opposition from Perkūnas is cosmic enmity, not governance. The Lauma / Laumė figure ([lauma_baltic.md](lauma_baltic.md)) is an ally in overlapping female-supernatural domains, not a superior. Within the witch hierarchy: Ragana (goddess) → raganos (lesser witch-beings) → mortal devotees / priestesses.

## Named individuals — Primary sources

Ragana is a singular divine archetype. No named sub-divine individuals below her are attested in primary Lithuanian or Latvian sources. The following represents the full extent of named evidence:

| Name | Source | Rank / role | Notes |
|------|--------|-------------|-------|
| Ragana | Lithuanian/Latvian *dainos*, oral tradition | Witch-goddess; cosmic balance-keeper; Winter Solstice sovereign | Singular divine archetype; the name also functions as the common noun for "witch" in both languages |
