# Ljósálfar — Norse Roster

> See also: [ljosalfar_norse.md](ljosalfar_norse.md)

## Command hierarchy

Álfheimr is ruled by Freyr (Vanir god of fertility), who received it as a tooth-gift at birth (*Grímnismál*, stanza 5). No internal Ljósálfar hierarchy or named leaders are documented in primary sources.

**Source priority:** Primary sources (Poetic Edda, Prose Edda) are authoritative.

## Named individuals — Primary sources

No individual Ljósálfar are named in primary Norse sources. The Ljósálfar are referred to exclusively as a collective.

> **Note:** The absence of named individuals reflects the sparseness of the primary record, not an absence of individuals in the mythology. Snorri Sturluson's account (c. 1220) is the only extended description of the Ljósálfar, and even it names no specific members of the race.
