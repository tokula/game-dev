# Eldjötnar — Norse Roster

> See also: [eldjotnar_norse.md](eldjotnar_norse.md)

## Command hierarchy

Surtr leads the Eldjötnar; he is described as their ruler and the guardian of Muspellsheimr. No internal hierarchy below Surtr is documented in primary sources — the fire giants are referenced collectively rather than as named individuals in battle.

At Ragnarök: Surtr leads the Múspellsmegir in their march across Bifröst toward Ásgarðr.

## Individual distinctions — Primary sources

**Surtr**
- `[Specialization-signature]` Wields a sword described as brighter than the sun; at Ragnarök he leads the Múspellsmegir, breaks Bifröst, and his fires engulf all Nine Realms — this is cosmological destruction, not a combat ability within the race's normal range — *Völuspá; Gylfaginning*
