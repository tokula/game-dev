# Veliona — Baltic Roster

> See also: [veliona_baltic.md](veliona_baltic.md)

## Command hierarchy

Veliona operates beneath Velnias (lord of the underworld) and above the Vėlės (souls of the dead) she guards. Her authority is custodial: she receives souls delivered by Giltinė and maintains their placement within the underworld realm that Velnias governs. No sub-beings are documented below Veliona in primary sources. The relationship between Veliona and Giltinė is complementary rather than hierarchical — Giltinė delivers the soul at death; Veliona receives and places it.

## Named individuals — Primary sources

Veliona is a singular divine being. No named subordinate beings or individual manifestations are documented in primary sources. Her attestation is sparse; the following represents the full extent of named evidence.
