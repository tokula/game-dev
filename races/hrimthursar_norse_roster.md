# Hrímþursar — Norse Roster

> See also: [hrimthursar_norse.md](hrimthursar_norse.md)

## Command hierarchy

No formal internal hierarchy is documented for the Hrímþursar as a race. Individual Jötnar of great power or wisdom command respect, but there is no attested king or single leader analogous to Surtr among the Eldjötnar. At Ragnarök they march collectively alongside the other Jötnar.

Above the Hrímþursar in the mythological order: the Æsir hold the divine order that the Hrímþursar structurally oppose, but there is no acknowledged superior relationship — only enmity.

## Individual distinctions — Primary sources

**Ymir / Aurgelmir**
- `[Specialization-signature]` His body *became* the world at creation — flesh became earth, blood became sea and lakes, bones became mountains and cliffs, skull became the sky, brain became clouds, eyebrows became the wall around Midgard. This is a cosmological function entirely outside the racial template for any living being — *Gylfaginning*
- `[Characteristic]` Self-generating: Ymir produced the first beings asexually, before any pairing existed. A male and female grew from his armpits; his feet begot a six-headed son.

**Bergelmir**
- `[Characteristic]` The only Hrímþursar to survive the flood of Ymir's blood when the Æsir slew Ymir; became the progenitor of the post-creation Jötunn lineage — *Vafþrúðnismál; Gylfaginning*
- `[Specialization-minor]` Possessed knowledge older than the gods themselves — Vafþrúðnir, the wisest of Jötnar, cites Bergelmir's era as the beginning of memory — *Vafþrúðnismál*
