# Hrímþursar — Norse Roster

> See also: [hrimthursar_norse.md](hrimthursar_norse.md)

## Command hierarchy

No formal internal hierarchy is documented among Hrímþursar beyond the genealogical succession Aurgelmir/Ymir → Þrúðgelmir → Bergelmir. Vafþrúðnir functions as a chieftain-level figure — he hosts Odin's wisdom contest and is treated as one of the foremost among the ancient jötnar — but no formal title is given. The Hrímþursar acknowledge no superior authority; they predate the gods and their realm lies outside the Æsir's dominion.

## Individual distinctions — Primary sources

**Aurgelmir / Ymir**
- `[Specialization-signature]` Self-reproduction without a mate — bore a man and woman from the pit of his armpit, and a son from his two feet entwining (*Gylfaginning*)
- `[Specialization-signature]` Cosmological substance — his body literally became the raw material of the world after his slaying by Odin's kin (*Gylfaginning*; *Völuspá*)

**Vafþrúðnir**
- `[Specialization-major]` Cosmic knowledge — holds first-hand knowledge of events from before creation through to Ragnarök; correctly answers on the origin of Niflheim, the sun and moon, the fate of the gods — defeated only by Odin's unanswerable question about his own whisper to the dead Baldr (*Vafþrúðnismál*)
