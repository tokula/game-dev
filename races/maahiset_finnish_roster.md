## Command hierarchy

No formal internal hierarchy is documented in primary sources. Maahiset appear collectively as a community of underground earth spirits (*väki*) sharing consistent behavioral patterns — rewarding courtesy, punishing neglect — but no named leaders, ranks, or authority structures are attested in the available open sources.
