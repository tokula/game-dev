## Command hierarchy

No formal internal hierarchy is documented for the maahiset in primary sources. They appear as a collective, egalitarian community of earth-dwellers. The only documented authority above them is Tapio's broader nature deity hierarchy:

- **Tapio** (forest deity, "King of the Forest") — governs the broader spirit ecology within which maahiset operate
  - **Maahiset** (collective) — independent earth-dwelling communities, each associated with specific territories; no internal ranking described in any primary source

No named leader, elder, or chief among the maahiset is attested in any primary source.
