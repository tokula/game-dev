# Dökkálfar — Norse Roster

> See also: [dokkalfar_norse.md](dokkalfar_norse.md)

## Command hierarchy

No hierarchy, ruler, or named individuals are documented for the Dökkálfar in primary sources. Snorri's account describes them only as a collective underground-dwelling people in a single paragraph.

**Source priority:** Primary sources (Prose Edda) are authoritative.

## Named individuals — Primary sources

No individual Dökkálfar are named in any primary Norse source. All available primary-source information on this race is contained in a single paragraph of Snorri Sturluson's *Gylfaginning* (c. 1220).

> **Note:** The near-total absence of named individuals and documented traits reflects the extreme sparseness of the primary record. Virtually all elaborated "Dark Elf" characters in games and fiction are original inventions with no primary-source counterpart.
