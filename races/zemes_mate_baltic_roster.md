# Zemes Māte — Baltic Roster

> See also: [zemes_mate_baltic.md](zemes_mate_baltic.md)

## Command hierarchy

Zemes māte operates within the Latvian *māte*-deity system, in which each divine mother governs her domain autonomously — there is no formal hierarchy among the mātes. She is beneath Dievs (sky god) in the overall cosmic order. Her chthonic aspects (Kapu māte, Veļu māte, Smilšu māte) are not subordinate beings but facets of the same divine presence manifesting in specialized domains. The dead (Veļi) are her charge as Veļu māte; no sub-hierarchy among the dead is documented as being within her authority rather than Velnias-equivalent governance in the Latvian tradition.

## Named individuals — Primary sources

Zemes māte is a singular divine presence with multiple aspects rather than a collective. The aspects themselves function as named manifestations:

| Name | Source | Rank / role | Notes |
|------|--------|-------------|-------|
| Zemes māte | *Latvju dainas*, Barons (1894–1915) | Earth Mother; primary form | The overarching earth-goddess; most frequently invoked aspect |
| Kapu māte | *Latvju dainas*, Barons (1894–1915) | Mother of Graves | Governs the grave-realm; protects properly buried dead |
| Veļu māte | *Latvju dainas*, Barons (1894–1915) | Mother of Dead Souls | Receives and rules the Latvian dead (veļi); presides over the White Sand Hill transit |
| Smilšu māte | *Latvju dainas*, Barons (1894–1915) | Mother of Sands | Associated with the White Sand Hill (*Smilšu kalns*) — the liminal landscape before the final crossing |

## Individual distinctions — Primary sources

**Veļu māte**
- `[Specialization-major]` Sole authority over the Latvian dead-souls realm — governs the veļi (dead souls) and the transit through the White Sand Hill; no other being shares this specific domain in Latvian cosmology

**Kapu māte**
- `[Specialization-minor]` Active protection of grave-sites — a violated or disturbed grave is a specific breach of her domain; she responds to desecration in ways that Zemes māte's general earth-authority does not mandate
