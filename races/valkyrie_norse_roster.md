# Valkyrie — Norse Roster

> See also: [valkyrie_norse.md](valkyrie_norse.md)

## Command hierarchy

```
Odin (Allfather — supreme commander; assigns missions, issues punishments)
└── Valkyries (divine shield-maidens; execute Odin's battlefield decrees)
    ├── Named / prominent — attested in primary texts
    └── Unnamed rank-and-file — implied as a larger collective by sources
```

No formal internal hierarchy is documented in the primary sources. Prominence is inferred from narrative role and frequency of mention across texts. Some Valkyries (Brynhildr, Sigrun) have full narrative arcs; most are names in a list.

## Sources

- *Völuspá*, Poetic Edda (c. 10th–13th century) — names six Valkyries; primary cosmological source
- *Grímnismál*, Poetic Edda — lists thirteen named Valkyries
- *Sigrdrífumál*, Poetic Edda — primary source for Brynhildr and Sigrdrífa
- *Helgakviða Hundingsbana I & II*, Poetic Edda — primary source for Sigrun
- *Völundarkviða*, Poetic Edda — source of the three swan-maiden Valkyries
- *Skáldskaparmál*, Prose Edda, Snorri Sturluson (c. 1220) — Hildr narrative and kenning lists
- *Gylfaginning*, Prose Edda, Snorri Sturluson — Eir and general Valkyrie role
- *Káruljóð* (largely lost) — Kára reference preserved in *Hrómundar saga Gripssonar*
