# Vėlės — Baltic Roster

> See also: [veles_baltic.md](veles_baltic.md)

## Command hierarchy

Vėlės are governed by Velnias (lord of the dead), who rules the realm they inhabit after death. No internal hierarchy among individual Vėlės is documented — they are equal shades in the afterlife realm, grouped with their family members rather than ranked by any authority structure.

Velnias (lord of the dead) → all Vėlės collectively
Each Vėlė ↔ living family descendants (mutual obligation relationship across the boundary of death)

**Source priority:** Primary sources (folklore, mythology, religious texts, historical accounts) are the authoritative origin. Games and fiction derive from them. Always populate `— Primary sources` first and most completely.

## Named individuals — Primary sources

No individual Vėlės are named in primary sources. By definition every dead human becomes a vėlė — they are a universal collective rather than a defined set of individuals. Primary sources describe the nature and behavior of vėlės in the aggregate through folk songs, lamentations, and ritual accounts, not through named members.
