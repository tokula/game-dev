# Velnias — Baltic Roster

> See also: [velnias_baltic.md](velnias_baltic.md)

## Command hierarchy

Velnias stands alone as sovereign of the Baltic underworld — he holds authority over all Vėlės (spirits of the dead), over the Kaukai (connected to his domain as souls of the dead), and over the subterranean deity Puskaitis. No being of equal standing is documented within his realm.

Velnias → all Vėlės collectively → Kaukai (via Puskaitis)
Giltinė operates as an autonomous agent of death, not under Velnias's direct command in primary sources — her relationship to him is complementary rather than hierarchical.

**Source priority:** Primary sources (folklore, mythology, religious texts, historical accounts) are the authoritative origin. Games and fiction derive from them. Always populate `— Primary sources` first and most completely.

**Section naming rule:** `— Primary sources` is used here as the authoritative record is a cultural tradition (Lithuanian/Latvian mythology), not a specific creative work.

## Individual distinctions — Primary sources

**Velnias**
- `[Specialization-signature]` Sole sovereign of the entire Lithuanian underworld — this absolute scope exceeds what the racial template implies for a mere chthonic deity
- `[Modifier]` Post-Christian conflation with the Devil (Piktis) overlaid his pre-Christian character — documented in Szyrwid (1629); treat all post-1387 descriptions with caution — *origin: Christianization of Lithuania*

**Giltinė**
- `[Specialization-major]` Kills through a poisonous tongue — venom collected from corpses; this is an individual weapon no other chthonic deity is attested to use
- `[Characteristic]` Transforms progressively from beauty to ghastly form over seven years in a coffin — a unique biographical change not shared with other chthonic beings
- `[Characteristic]` Manifests as an owl as her sacred animal; her hooting is a death omen

**Puskaitis**
- `[Characteristic]` Lives exclusively beneath the elder bush — uniquely site-bound among chthonic deities; his authority does not extend beyond that specific subterranean domain
